글 빈칸 Curious, observant, and amazed by the beauty of wide-open spaces, a strong taste for challenges and a hunger to experience them intensely, Mathieu embodies true adventurous spirit.

Born in Tarbes at the foot of the Pyrenees, Mathieu grew up with an innate connection to nature, leading to an extraordinary career in snowboarding. A four-time world champion and two-time Olympian, Mathieu has now shifted his focus to all-terrain adventures, seamlessly blending snowboarding, surfing, and environmental advocacy. His ventures span from riding waves in Hawaii to snowboarding under the Northern Lights in Norway. Beyond his athletic prowess, Mathieu is deeply committed to raising environmental awareness, particularly on issues like glacier melting. As an ambassador of the Water Family, he inspires the next generation to protect our planet through the power of outdoor sports.
